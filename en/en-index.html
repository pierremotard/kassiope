Our project this year in Ahmedabad India

  • Our project will take place between January and March 2018
  • Continue to carry out its eye tests and distribute glasses
  • Offer cataract surgery to the communities associated with Manav Sadhna
  • Deepen local doctors’ knowledge about pathologies associated to eyes
  • Help create a center in order to teach doctors how to perform cataract surgeries through the Lotus Eye Clinic’s ophtalmologists
  • Initiate a project that is both socially and economically sustainable

Let's talk about us !

  • Kassiopé is a non-lucrative association aimed at offering free eye care to the most disabled
  • Two of us, having already been to India and taught in one of the schools near the Gandhi Ashram, imagined a project of offering free eye tests and glasses to the numerous students, especially children
  • We all share the ambition : Travel to India, together, for the purpose of offering to children a decent quality of life, beginning by a suitable eyesight.
